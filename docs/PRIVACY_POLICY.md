# ScanForge Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 20 June 2026_

ScanForge is built **on-device first**. Your documents, scans, and recognized text are processed and
stored **locally on your device**. We do not operate servers that receive your document content, and
we do not sell or share your personal data.

## What ScanForge does on your device

- **Scanning & image enhancement** — Camera capture, edge detection, perspective correction, and
  filters run entirely on your device.
- **Text recognition (OCR)** — Performed on-device using bundled, offline ML Kit models. The text we
  extract from your pages never leaves your device as part of OCR.
- **Storage** — Documents, pages, thumbnails, recognized text, tags, and folders are stored in a
  private app database and private app storage on your device.

## Data we do **not** collect

- We do not collect your documents, images, or recognized text.
- We do not require an account, and we do not collect your name, email, contacts, or location.
- We do not use advertising identifiers or third-party advertising/tracking SDKs.

## Optional features that leave your device — only if you turn them on

All of the following are **off by default** and require an explicit action by you:

| Feature | What leaves the device | Where it goes |
|---|---|---|
| **Export / Share** | The specific document you export or share | The destination you pick (Files, another app, etc.) |
| **Cloud sync** _(experimental, off by default)_ | Document data you choose to sync | The cloud provider you connect. _Not configured in this build._ |
| **Crash reports** _(opt-in)_ | Anonymous crash diagnostics (stack traces, device model, app version). **No document content.** | The crash-reporting backend, when configured. The shipped build records nothing off-device. |
| **Anonymous usage** _(opt-in)_ | Coarse, anonymous feature-usage counts (e.g. "an export completed"). **No titles, text, or images.** | The analytics backend, when configured. The shipped build records nothing off-device. |

You can turn any of these off at any time in **Settings → Privacy**.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — Used only to capture pages while you are scanning. You can use ScanForge without the
  camera by importing existing images or PDFs.

ScanForge requests no storage permission; importing and exporting use the Android Storage Access
Framework, so you choose exactly which files are read or written.

## Data retention & deletion

- Deleting a document moves it to Trash; emptying Trash (or the auto-purge after your configured
  retention period) permanently deletes it and its image files from your device.
- Uninstalling the app removes all ScanForge data from your device.

## Children's privacy

ScanForge is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

We will update this document and the "Last updated" date when our practices change.
